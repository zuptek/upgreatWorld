Precision Fabrication & High-Quality Branding Production

At UpGreat World, we believe that great design deserves great execution. Our in-house Production and Fabrication teams are some of the most skilled in the country. We don't just print; we build monuments to your brand's excellence. From the smallest desk signage to the largest rooftop hoarding structure, every piece we produce is a testament to quality.

We work with state-of-the-art Large-Format Printing technology and a wide array of premium materials including high-grade ACP, durable LED modules, automotive-grade paints, and high-tensile steel for structures. Whether it's a nationwide retail rollout or a custom-built experiential kiosk, we provide a seamless DESIGN-to-BUILD journey.

Key Highlights

  • Architectural Signage: 3D LED letters, pylon signs, and pylon signage for corporate parks.
  • Large-Format Printing: UV printing on vinyl, fabric, and flex for high-vibrancy OOH assets.
  • Custom Fabrication: Bespoke metal, wood, and acrylic structures for events and retail.
  • Structure Maintenance: Regular auditing and repair of existing outdoor advertising assets.
  • In-Shop Displays: Designing and building innovative product displays and shelving units.
