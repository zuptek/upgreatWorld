Direct Engagement with BTL Marketing

Below-the-Line (BTL) marketing is for brands that want to create an immediate impact and drive direct results. While OOH builds awareness, BTL builds trust through personal interaction. At UpGreat World, we bring years of experience in managing complex ground-level campaigns across India.

From Mobile Van Roadshows that cover entire districts to Mall Activations that target weekend shoppers, we handle everything — the creative concept, the fabrication of stalls and raths, the hiring of promoters, and the final on-ground management. We ensure your brand doesn't just look good; it feels alive.

Our BTL Services:

  • Mall & Multiplex Activations: Interactive kiosks and stalls in high-footfall shopping zones.
  • Society RWA Branding: Engaging residents inside their housing complexes through activations.
  • Canopy & Stall Setups: High-visibility stalls at markets, metro stations, and local hubs.
  • In-Shop Branding: Transforming retail environments into powerful marketing zones.

BTL Marketing FAQ

What is the difference between ATL and BTL? expand_more
ATL (Above-the-Line) is for mass awareness (billboards, TV), while BTL (Below-the-Line) is for targeted, direct engagement with specific customer groups.
Can you handle roadshows in smaller towns? expand_more
Yes, we specialize in Tier-2 and Tier-3 town roadshows, helping brands penetrate deeper markets across all Indian states.
