Digital OOH Advertising FAQ

What is DOOH advertising? expand_more
DOOH (Digital Out-of-Home) is any digital media that is located in public spaces, such as digital billboards, LED walls, and smart screens in airports or malls.
Can I run multiple creatives on DOOH? expand_more
Yes! You can rotate multiple creatives or run different ads for morning and evening shifts to maximize audience relevance.
