Lifestyle Marketing with Mall & Cinema Advertising

Malls and Multiplexes are the modern cathedrals of consumption. They attract an audience with high disposable income and a high willingness to spend. At UpGreat World, we specialize in high-impact lifestyle marketing that creates deep brand resonance in these premium environments.

Our Mall Branding solutions include everything from Atrium Dominations and Lift Branding to Floor Stickers and Branded Kiosks. In Multiplexes, we offer On-Screen Cinema Ads during blockbusters, as well as Lobby Branding and Seat-Back Sponsorships. We ensure your brand is unmissable from the moment a shopper enters the mall to the moment they leave the cinema.

Key Highlights

  • Atrium & Pillar Dominations: Massive, high-visibility branding in the mall's central hub.
  • Elevator & Escalator Wraps: High-frequency impressions in captive movement zones.
  • Mall Kiosks & Stall Setups: Directly engaging shoppers at the point of sale.
  • Multiplex On-Screen Ads: Captivating a captive audience with high-res cinema screen spots.
  • Lobby & Concourse Branding: High-impact backlit signage and digital screens in multiplex lobbies.
