Outdoor Advertising FAQ

What is OOH advertising? expand_more
OOH or Out-of-Home advertising refers to any advertising that reaches consumers while they are outside their homes. This includes billboards, transit media, and street furniture.
How much does a hoarding cost in India? expand_more
Hoarding costs vary significantly based on location, size, and duration. A premium site in Delhi or Mumbai may cost several lakhs per month, while sites in smaller cities are more affordable. Contact us for a custom quote.
How do you measure the reach of a billboard? expand_more
We use traffic volume data, location intelligence, and audience demography metrics to estimate the monthly impressions for each OOH site.
