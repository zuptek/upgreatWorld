DOOH FAQ
What is the difference between OOH and DOOH?
Traditional OOH (Out-of-Home) advertising uses static printed formats like hoardings and
posters. DOOH (Digital Out-of-Home) uses digital screens that can display dynamic, animated
content, run multiple ads, and be updated remotely in real time.
Can DOOH campaigns be targeted by time of day or audience?
Yes. Programmatic DOOH campaigns can be scheduled by time of day (dayparting), day of the
week, weather conditions, and audience demographics — allowing precise targeting not
possible with traditional static OOH.
